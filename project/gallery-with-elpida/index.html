Gallery with ... "ELPIDA"

Apr 27, 2012

Gallery with “ELPIDA”, (English translation: Gallery with HOPE) was a 3-day art exhibition, organised as a joined venture of Ioannis Giannakoulis, Alexandros Kampis and I that took place in Moraitis School aimed to raise funds for the Association of Friends and Children with Cancer “ELPIDA” while simultaneously supporting up-and-comming artists from the 1st workshop of Athens School of Fine Arts.

In total, 152 art pieces were sold raising almost €75,000. The project would not have been possible without the kind contribution of the Piraeus Bank Group Cultural Foundation.
